    About Us

    SG electronics Pte Ltd. is one of the industry's leading Stocking Distributors of Electronic, Semiconductor and Electro Mechanical Components based in Singapore.
    We support customers with qualified parts and competitive prices within short span of time.
    The company obtains factory-new inventory and sells it on to OEM, EMS, CMs, and other leading stockiest

    Electronic Component Sourcing

    Components purchased from Manufacturer directly / Authorised distributors / Open Market / OEM & EMS inventory

    Supply chain Solutions

    Material sourcing & Management / Last time buy / Cost reduction / immediate or short-leadtime to deliver the goods

    Open Market Risk Mitigation

    Follow stringent vendor qualification process to avoid counterfeit parts & ensure qualified service through inspection, utilizing our equipment and certifications (ISO 9001-2015)

    Vendor Management

    Vendor consolidation results in cost reduction, reduce overhead cost by limiting multiple vendors to one vendor (SG Electronics), full traceability is maintained

    Excess Inventory from OEM / EMS customers can utilize to match customer's unique needs.
